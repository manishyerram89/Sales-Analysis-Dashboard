# Sales Performance Dashboard

This repository contains a Power BI dashboard that provides a detailed breakdown of sales performance across months, salespeople, and teams. It highlights actual vs. target sales, variance, and progress toward reaching sales goals.

![Dashboard Preview](salesdashboard.png)

---

## üìä Dashboard Highlights

### **1. Total Sales Actual**
- Displays **$19M** total actual sales.
- Provides an at-a-glance summary of overall business performance.

### **2. Total Sales Target**
- Shows the target of **$19M**, allowing direct comparison to actual results.

### **3. Variance & Variance %**
- Shows a variance of **($367K)** below target.
- Variance percentage is **1.9%**, indicating a slight shortfall in reaching goals.

### **4. Monthly Sales Trend**
- Bar chart visualizing monthly sales from Jan 2023 to Jan 2024.
- Only **2 out of 14 months** met or exceeded targets.
- Helps identify seasonal performance and declining or improving months.

### **5. Salesperson Performance Table**
- Includes:
  - Salesperson picture
  - Total Sales Actual
  - Total Sales Target
  - Variance %
  - Sparkline trend (Actual sales over time)
- Great for evaluating individual team member contribution and consistency.

### **6. Target Status by Date**
- Column chart showing days when targets were met or missed.
- Useful for monitoring pacing against targets.

### **7. Team Overview**
- Displays four teams:
  - **Delish**
  - **Tempo**
  - **Jucies**
  - **Yummies**
- Allows segmentation of performance within team structures.
